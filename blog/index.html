How the journey started One of my favorite Linux distros overall is without any doubt Manjaro, a Linux distribution which derivates from ArchLinux. Some days ago, annoyed by the restricted Apple macOS, I decided to install Manjaro on my MacBook Pro 13' 2017 base model. This decision was also made because of the continuous and loud sound of the fan; Yes fan and not fans because this particular model has only one fan to cool down the entire computer.
